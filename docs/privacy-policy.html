People Memory

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

People Memory stores all your data exclusively on your device. We collect nothing. We send nothing. Your contacts stay yours.

1. What Data We Collect

We collect no data whatsoever.

People Memory is a fully offline application. All information you enter — including people's names, notes, tags, trust levels, dates, and photos — is stored only on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage on mobile, browser localStorage on web).

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Mobile (iOS & Android): Data is stored using AsyncStorage, a local key-value store on your device.
  • Web: Data is stored in your browser's localStorage.

No data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. No Internet Required

People Memory does not require an internet connection to function. The app operates fully offline. There is no backend server, no API calls to remote services, and no analytics or telemetry.

4. Photos

If you choose to add a photo to a contact profile, that photo is stored only on your device. Photos are never uploaded, synced, or shared with anyone.

5. PIN Lock

The optional PIN feature stores a SHA-256 hash of your PIN locally on your device. The actual PIN value is never stored anywhere — only its cryptographic hash. This hash never leaves your device.

6. Third-Party Services

People Memory does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Crash reporting services
  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud sync services

7. Children's Privacy

People Memory does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. There is nothing to collect.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection without clearly communicating that change.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via our GitHub repository.
